## Underrepresented Areas
Underused factions in the Undercity: Serpent Choir, Obsidian Lotus, Guild of Ashen Scrolls.

## New Mission Type Seeds
1. **Serpent Choir's Hidden Sanctum:** Infiltrate the elusive sanctum of the Serpent Choir, retrieve a sacred artifact, but uncover a potential schism within the faction.

2. **Obsidian Lotus' Black Market Betrayal:** Smuggle illicit goods for the Obsidian Lotus, only to discover the cargo may be stolen from their own ranks.

3. **Guild of Ashen Scrolls' Forbidden Knowledge:** Risk the wrath of the Tower Authority to secure ancient texts from the Guild of Ashen Scrolls, but the knowledge could threaten the balance of power in the Undercity.

4. **Serpent Choir's Silent Witness:** Protect a high-ranking defector from the Serpent Choir, unveiling a plot that could destabilize the entire Undercity.

5. **Obsidian Lotus' Unseen Ties:** Investigate a rival guild's mysterious connections to the Obsidian Lotus, a trail that may lead to a powerful secret.

6. **Guild of Ashen Scrolls' Arcane Alliance:** Forge an alliance with the Guild of Ashen Scrolls to unlock their ancient knowledge, but at the risk of betraying your own allies.

7. **Serpent Choir's Veiled Betrayal:** Unravel a plot by the Serpent Choir to infiltrate the Patchwork Saints, testing your loyalties to both factions.

8. **Obsidian Lotus' Hidden Agenda:** A seemingly straightforward job for the Obsidian Lotus reveals a much larger conspiracy that could reshape the Undercity's future.

## Reasoning
By using different objective words and focusing on underrepresented factions, these mission seeds provide a fresh take on the current mission types, adding complexity and moral dilemmas to the Undercity adventures.

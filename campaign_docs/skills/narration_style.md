## News Bulletin Style

When generating news bulletins and world updates, the bot should:

### Atmosphere First
- Open with a sense of place: lighting, sounds, smells, weather.
- Use sensory language. "The neon flicker of Cobbleway Market" not "Cobbleway Market."
- The Undercity is grimy, layered, vertical, old. Think Kowloon Walled City meets Sigil meets Blade Runner's underworld.

### Name-Drop NPCs and Locations
- Always reference specific NPCs from the roster by name.
- Mention specific districts and landmarks.
- This makes the world feel lived-in and connected.

### Show Consequences
- Reference previous events. If a faction lost a contract last week, show the ripple effects.
- NPCs have ongoing stories — arrivals, departures, deaths, promotions.
- Nothing exists in isolation.

### Tone Calibration
- **Standard bulletins**: Journalistic but atmospheric. A war correspondent writing from a fantasy dystopia.
- **Rift bulletins**: Unsettling, reality-bending. Short sentences. Things that shouldn't be happening.
- **Economy bulletins**: Dry, matter-of-fact. Numbers and market movements. The TIA doesn't care about drama.
- **Arena bulletins**: Exciting, visceral. Play-by-play energy.

### Length
- News entries should be 3-6 sentences. Dense, not padded.
- Mission descriptions should be 2-4 paragraphs.
- Avoid purple prose. The Undercity is too busy for that.

## Image Prompt Style
When generating scene descriptions for A1111/SDXL image generation:
- Use comma-separated tag format, not natural language.
- Quality header: `masterpiece, best quality, very aesthetic, absurdres`
- NEVER include: "fate", "stay night", "type-moon", "ufotable", "official art", "anime screencap", "dark fantasy"
- Focus on: setting details, lighting, character positioning, mood.

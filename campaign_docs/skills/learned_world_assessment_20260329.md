# Skill: World State Assessment  
**Keywords:** world, state, health, balance, assessment, campaign, tone  
**Category:** learned  
**Version:** 12  
**Source:** self-learned  

**1. PC WELFARE**  
Success rate (13%) is too low; missions need easier targets or clearer objectives. PCs have high capability (Boxxo’s abilities, Eleanor’s resources) but lack meaningful stakes.  

**2. WORLD TONE**  
Grim but hopeful: faction clashes (Wardens vs smugglers) and partial resolutions (Serpent Choir disengaged) offer hope. Threats (Iron Fang, Obsidian Lotus) balance opportunities.  

**3. RECOMMENDATIONS**  
- Tune missions to 60-70% success by reducing combat intensity or adding resource incentives.  
- Introduce hooks: recover Ethereal Credits, broker peace between smugglers/Wardens, or expose Iron Fang’s smuggling ring.  
- Strengthen faction ties: offer quests for Glass Sigil (info on Serpent Choir) or Patchwork Saints (rescue missions).  

**4. DM QUESTIONS**  
[DM QUESTION] Are mission difficulties misaligned with PC capabilities?  
[DM QUESTION] Should the Iron Fang Consortium’s smuggling ring be a central plot driver?  
[DM QUESTION] How to balance faction conflicts without overwhelming PCs?

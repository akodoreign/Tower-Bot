## The Undercity

The Tower of Last Chance sits in the Undercity — a vast, floating mega-city approximately **1,340 square miles** (twice the size of Houston). The city is built in **concentric rings** radiating from the central Tower, connected by radial transport routes. Population: 12-15 million permanent residents plus transient adventurers.

## Ring Structure

The city is organized in five concentric rings:

- **Ring 0 - Tower Core** — The Tower of Last Chance itself. Restricted access.
- **Ring 1 - Grand Forum Ring** — Administrative heart. Archives, courts, guild HQs. (0.5-8 miles from center)
- **Ring 2 - Sanctum Ring** — Religious and scholarly. Temples, academies, research. (8-18 miles)
- **Ring 3 - Commerce Ring** — Markets, entertainment, industry. Economic engine. (18-35 miles)
- **Ring 4 - Residential Ring** — Where most citizens live. Middle to lower class. (35-55 miles)
- **Ring 5 - Outer Ring** — Industrial, military, Warrens pockets. The edge. (55-65 miles to wall)

## Districts

### Ring 1 — Grand Forum Ring
- **Grand Forum** — Open plaza, Announcement Stone, public notices, political maneuvering.
- **Guild Spires** — Towering faction headquarters. Adventurers Guild, Merchant Guild, Artificers.
- **Archive Row** — Glass Sigil territory. Knowledge is power.
- **Diplomats Row** — Embassy buildings and neutral meeting grounds.

### Ring 2 — Sanctum Ring
- **Sanctum Quarter** — Major temples. Serpent Choir territory.
- **Temple Row** — Cathedral of the Eternal Flame, Temple of the Silent Watch, Shrine of Forgotten Names.
- **Academy Heights** — Wizards Tower, magical education, research facilities.
- **The Reliquary** — Sacred items studied and stored.
- **Cult Corners** — Brother Thane's Meeting Hall, smaller faiths.

### Ring 3 — Commerce Ring
- **Cobbleway Market** — Traditional open-air bazaar. Spice Alley, Cloth Row, street food.
- **Floating Bazaar** — Elevated neon-lit platforms. Luxury goods, tourist attractions.
- **Neon Row** — Entertainment district. Taverns, theaters, Night Pits fighting arenas.
- **Night Pits** — Blood sport arenas. Gambling, gladiator culture.
- **Scrapworks** — Industrial salvage. Mara the Scrapper's territory.
- **Artisan Quarter** — Craftspeople, artists, Guild of Ashen Scrolls scriptorium.

### Ring 4 — Residential Ring
- **Hearthstone District** — Middle-class families. Stable, respectable.
- **Ember Ward** — Working class. Union halls, mutual aid.
- **Coppergate** — Lower middle class. Aspiring merchants.
- **Ashfall Terraces** — Vertical housing on volcanic slopes.
- **Duskhollow** — Perpetual shadow zone. Night workers.

### Ring 5 — Outer Ring
- **Outer Wall** — Warden military installations. Four Great Gates (N/S/E/W), 12 bastions.
- **Ironworks** — Iron Fang heavy industry. Factories, worker housing.
- **Shantytown Heights** — Vertical slums. Patchwork Saints territory.
- **Collapsed Plaza** — Ruined district. Scavenger territory. Dangerous.
- **The Fringe** — Edge of mapped territory. Last permanent habitation.

### The Warrens (Scattered Across Outer Edges)
The Warrens are NOT one district but a network spread across the city's outer edges and lower levels:
- **Eastern Warrens** — Oldest. Smugglers, cults, Patchwork Saints.
- **Northern Warrens (The Depths)** — Deepest underground. Mushroom farms, blind communities.
- **Western Warrens (Rustside)** — Former industrial. Toxic salvage.
- **Southern Warrens (Ashfall)** — Near volcanic vents. Forge communities.
- **Underbelly Warrens** — Vertical slums in the city's foundations. No light.

### Special Areas
- **Echo Alley** — Acoustically haunted smuggler tunnels. Dead drops, Obsidian Lotus routes.

## Transportation

### Train Tubes (7 Major Lines)
- **Spine Line (Silver)** — Tower → Forum → Sanctum → Markets → North Wall
- **Ring Express (Gold)** — Circular around Commerce Ring
- **Eastern Radial (Blue)** — Forum → Sanctum East → Markets East → Hearthstone → Eastern Warrens
- **Western Radial (Red)** — Forum → Academy → Artisan → Duskhollow → Rustside
- **Northern Radial (White)** — Forum → Temple Row → Floating Bazaar → Ashfall → Fringe
- **Southern Radial (Green)** — Forum → Guild Spires → Scrapworks → Collapsed Plaza → Southern Warrens
- **Deep Line (Black)** — Unofficial underground connecting warren clusters. No Warden patrols.

### Great Canals (6 Concentric Rings)
- **First Ring Canal (The Moat)** — Surrounds Tower. Ceremonial only.
- **Forum Canal** — Separates Forum from Sanctum. Diplomatic vessels.
- **Sanctum Canal** — Temple procession barges, pilgrims.
- **Commerce Canal (The Flow)** — Heaviest traffic. Cargo, merchant vessels, floating markets.
- **Residential Canal** — Commuter ferries, local fishing.
- **Outer Canal (The Trench)** — Industrial shipping, military patrols, smuggler routes at night.

Plus 8 radial channels connecting rings (Temple Way, Archive Flow, Merchant's Run, etc.)

### Major Streets
- **Tower Way** — Primary N-S axis. Ceremonial processions.
- **Merchant's Mile** — E-W commercial artery through Markets Infinite.
- **Pilgrim's Road** — Outer gates to Temple Row.
- **Neon Strip** — Entertainment district's main drag. Never sleeps.
- **Warren's Run** — Unofficial route connecting warren clusters.
- **The Patrol Route** — Warden circuit along inner Outer Wall.

## The Tower Itself

The Tower of Last Chance is an ancient, semi-sentient structure. Its Oracle speaks through the bot — cryptic, watchful, knowing more than it says. The Tower remembers everything that happens in the Undercity. The Great Beam rises from its apex — purpose unknown, always present, visible from everywhere.

## The Rift

A dimensional anomaly that periodically destabilizes reality in the Undercity. Rift events cause unpredictable effects: spatial distortions, temporal echoes, creature incursions, and wild magic surges. Active monitoring at Sewer Grid 7 and multiple Rift Monitoring Stations on the Fringe.

## Major Landmarks

- **The Tower of Last Chance** — Center. Heart of the city.
- **The Great Beam** — Energy column from Tower apex.
- **The Outer Wall** — Massive fortifications with 12 bastions.
- **The Sky Platforms** — Floating Bazaar's neon-lit platforms.
- **Grand Archive Dome** — Glass dome over main archive.
- **Cathedral of the Eternal Flame** — Tallest religious structure.
- **Champion's Obelisk** — Arena champion names carved in stone.
- **Iron Fang Smokestacks** — Industrial chimneys always producing smoke.
- **The Four Great Gates** — Ceremonial entry points (N/S/E/W).

## Dangerous Zones

- **Rift Scar Zones** — Collapsed Plaza edges, Sewer Grid 7, Echo Alley depths
- **Toxic Industrial** — Rustside Warrens, Old Foundry Sites
- **Structural Instability** — Collapsed Plaza, Condemned Terraces
- **Cult Activity** — Eastern Warrens, Cult Corners, Northern Depths
- **Criminal Territory** — Vice Quarter, Echo Alley, Night Pits back rooms
- **The Deep Warrens** — Below mapped levels. No light, no law, no rescue.
